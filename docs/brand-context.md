# Sproutflow Studio  
**Turning small business ambitions into high-impact online experiences**

## What It Is
Sproutflow Studio is a **boutique web design and digital strategy agency** focused on empowering **small and growing businesses** to compete with enterprise-level quality without requiring enterprise-level budgets. The studio blends design, strategy, and technical execution to create websites that are not only visually compelling but intentionally built to **drive measurable business outcomes**.

## Core Vision
Sproutflow exists to **level the digital playing field for small businesses**. Rather than offering cookie-cutter templates or surface-level design, the studio applies **enterprise thinking, conversion psychology, and strategic clarity** to every engagement. The philosophy emphasizes partnership, transparency, and long-term growth over one-off builds.

## Target Clients
Sproutflow primarily serves:
- Solo founders and early-stage businesses seeking credibility and market validation  
- Growing companies ready to differentiate from competitors  
- Established small businesses modernizing or rebuilding their digital presence  
- Brands where the website plays a **direct role in revenue, trust, and lead generation**

## Services & Offerings
Sproutflow provides structured, tiered packages to meet clients at different stages of maturity.

### Starter ($850–$1,500)
- Template-based design with thoughtful customization  
- Fast turnaround and professional polish  
- Ideal for early-stage businesses needing a credible online presence  

### Foundation ($2,000–$2,800)
- Semi-custom design with strategic discovery  
- Clear information architecture and messaging  
- Designed to support future growth and marketing efforts  

### Growth ($3,500–$5,500)
- Fully custom design and build  
- Performance optimization and SEO foundations  
- Built to capture demand, improve conversions, and scale  

### Market Leader ($7,500+)
- Advanced custom solutions  
- Deep strategic positioning and conversion strategy  
- For businesses where the website is a **primary growth engine**

### Add-On Services
- SEO strategy and optimization  
- Professional copywriting and content strategy  
- Ongoing maintenance and care plans  
- Performance monitoring and iteration support  

## Approach & Process
Sproutflow follows a clear, collaborative, and transparent workflow:

1. **Discovery Call**  
   Focused on business goals, audience, and success metrics before design decisions.

2. **Proposal & Planning**  
   Clear scope, timeline, and pricing with no ambiguity.

3. **Design & Build**  
   Iterative development with staging previews and structured client feedback.

4. **Launch & Handoff**  
   White-glove launch, documentation, and training so clients feel confident managing their site.

5. **Ongoing Support (Optional)**  
   Continuous optimization, updates, and growth-oriented improvements.
