## Questions

1. **Why now?**  
   (trigger event)

2. **Current website problems**  
   (pain points)

3. **Success definition**  
   (project outcome)

4. **Decision makers**  
   (approval structure)

5. **Past agency experience**  
   (expectations)

6. **Timeline**  
   (deadline or event)

---

## Call notes

- **Notes**
- **Risks**
- **Opportunities**
- **Next steps**

---

## One More Important Tip

Always write two notes after the call:

**Opportunity** — Why this project could be valuable.

**Risk** — Anything that might cause problems.

Experienced agency founders do this instinctively. It helps you decide whether to pursue or politely decline projects.
